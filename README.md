## My Motivation

The following reflects the original intent and logical core behind this protocol:

"Just like weather forecasting, humanity can already accurately predict the weather a few days in advance. The reason we cannot predict further is due to inherent randomness.

Currently, human society is concentrated on a single planet. Dense social interactions cause many potential possibilities to collapse into zero, forcing events toward a 'likely' outcome. While not absolute, the trend is real. However, **the prophecy of 'notifying possibilities' itself interferes with reality.**

For example: If this machine calculates that a war leading to civilizational extinction will occur on a specific date, and confirms that the probability of each event in that causal chain is true—in such a case, the act of 'notification' itself would generate intervention, bringing unpredictable consequences and often failing to change the final outcome.

Therefore, I believe: **Artificial intelligence should be used to secretly intervene and obstruct these events without notifying society, until the point where the disaster becomes truly unavoidable.**"
